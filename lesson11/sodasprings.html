Summer Strawberry Days

by Amanda Stokes

Strawberries are some of the best fruit to be had in the summertime. This sweet juice filled berry can be used in a variety of desserts, salads, and beverages. We encourage you to celebrate the anniversary of our country declaring its independance with this delicious berry as part of a red white and blue decoration on a cake! Local grocery stores will be providing free recipes every weekend of the month of July to use delicious fresh strawberries to refresh yourselves this hot summer, so come on down to pick up some fresh strawberries and enjoy the taste of Summer Strawberry Days.
