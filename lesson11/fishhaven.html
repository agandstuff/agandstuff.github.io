Masking the Mayhem

by Amanda Stokes

2020 has certainly been a year that no one expected. With the outbreak of COVID-19 there arose mass hysteria, extreme exodus of supplies from grocery stores, and high levels of confusion over the truth behind the disease. There are some who even say the pandemic falls under the category of a conspiracy theory to hide government activity. While it is difficult for many of us around the world to understand what is really going on, we here at RainCasters like to err on the side of caution. We have partnered with town leaders in Fish Haven to provide free supplies and face masks to those in need in order to function in daily life. Please visit the town home page for more information.
