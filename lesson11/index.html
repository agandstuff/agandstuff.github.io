Stormwatchers Guide

by Amanda Stokes

Growing up in a smaller town, my family used to go to the attic of our home, which had this great big window, and look out to watch the thunderstorms to see the crashing lightning come down to meet the earth. To this day, we enjoy the opportunities to jump in the car and chase after thunderstorms, a la Twister, and try to catch the biggest arcs of electricity across the sky.

Join us as we explore various tips, tricks, and hints to enjoing the various glorious events that nature brings, while also approaching these phenomenon in a safe method. Keep visiting for further articles to enjoy all that Mother Nature has to offer!
