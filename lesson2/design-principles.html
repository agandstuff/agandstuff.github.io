Design Principles Document

Amanda G. Stokes

White Space

Ancestry | ancestry.com

Ancestry exemplifies the white space and clean design principle through their minimal images, and ample amount of space surrounding them. They show an even cleaner and simple design by adding white space between the images.

Contrast

Apple | apple.com

Apple has a primarily black and white color scheme, showing stark contrast between the page header and the main part of the page. This is echoed further with a black section advertising one of their latest products, followed again by a light gray/white section.

Visual Hierarchy

Codecademy | codecademy.com

Codecademy shows visual hierarchy on their home page, with the largest section dedicated to a form to log in or sign up to an account on their site. This shows that it is the most important part of the page, with other items including social links, dropdowns and links to legal documents being quite smaller.
