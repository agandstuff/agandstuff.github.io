Site Purpose

The purpose of the RainCasters site is to invite people to become more aware of their local weather forecasts. RainCasters is geared towards helping people be prepared for future weather events and to find the joy in the various types of weather that we experience, through activity ideas for inclement weather and when to stay safe at home during dangerous occasions.

The domain for the site will be raincasters.com and will include a page of the local forecast, as well as a page of links to useful activities for rainy days and advice for keeping safe when dangerous weather conditions are present.
