Target Audience

The target audience will include young adults, married or single, who may also have young children, these individuals lead active lives and enjoy various activities indoors and outdoors.

One example of an individual who would visit the site is a woman in her 30’s with 2 children between 5 and 10. School is out for the summer and they live in an area where it frequently rains. This consumer would visit the site to check the weather for the next week and check for ideas of things for her kids to do either indoors or outdoors.

Another consumer example would be a young man who is organizing a large outside event for friends and the community. He needs to monitor the weather for the next two weeks leading up to the even to determine if the back-up venue for his event will be required.

Sample Scenarios

Others may want to visit the site for reasons such as:

  • Do I need an umbrella for my commute?
  • What should I wear on my date this evening, including whether or not I need a jacket?
  • Is it going to rain during my camping trip?
  • Are there any outdoor activities for my kids when it’s raining?
  • What do I do during a hailstorm?

These are some examples of various questions where answers could be found on this site.
