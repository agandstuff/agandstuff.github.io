Typography

The type chosen for this site shows simplicity and comfort in the design. The main body paragraph text uses Open Sans, an easy to read sans-serif font. The header of the page with the site name and tag line use Pacifico, a font in the display family, to add contrast to the simple Open Sans, and show movement through the cursive to emphasize the point of the tag line, “Know When to Dance in the Rain.”
